About me

My name is Jonathon Bisiach, born in Adelaide, Australia. After leaving school I studied a Bachelor of Applied Science (Architectural Science) at Northern Territory University (now Charles Darwin University) in Darwin, and then later a Bachelor Arts (Professional Writing) at Victoria University in Melbourne. I spent most of my career in Australia working in the public service, particularly in the Department of Justice.

After meeting my future-wife, a Swede travelling in Australia in 1997, I moved to Sweden in 2013 and we were married in 2014.

As long as I can remember, we always had a computer in our house as my father used one for work. If memory serves me right, the first one I remember using was an IBM 386 with a turbo button.

In my spare time I enjoy reading comics, brewing and drinking beer, and am an avid gamer. I partuclarly enjoy space combat games, turn-based strategy, and well-written RPGs such as Dragon Age: Origins, The Elder Scrolls and The Witcher series.

Through participating in this course I hope I can gain this skills to make a website as good as the famous lingscars.com.
